A new layer of intelligence,
perpendicular to your striving.

OGI does not predict your future, plan your day, or replace your judgement. It sits at a right angle to all of that — clarifying, never commanding.

It clarifies.

The dominant intelligences of the last age were vertical — built to climb above you, predict your future, optimise your day, and eventually replace your judgement. OGI moves at a right angle to all of that.

It reads only what it needs. It returns only what you can act on. It surfaces, at most, three honest actions for the hour you are in — and falls silent the moment they are taken.
